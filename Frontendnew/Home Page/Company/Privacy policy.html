Privacy Policy

Your focus is your business. Your data is your property.

1. Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a personalized study experience. This includes your name, email, and study session statistics to power your Analytics Dashboard.

2. How We Use Data

We use your study habits to generate AI-crafted study plans and visualize your "Activity Aura." We never sell your data to third parties. Our business is focus, not advertising.

3. Security

Aura Study uses industry-standard encryption for all data transfers. Your passwords are hashed and stored securely. We prioritize your digital safety so you can prioritize your work.
