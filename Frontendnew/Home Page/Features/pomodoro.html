Aura Pomodoro

An online Pomodoro Timer to boost your productivity.

What is Aura Pomodoro?

Aura Pomodoro is a customizable pomodoro timer integrated directly into our Study Sanctuaries. The aim is to help you focus on any task you are working on, such as study, writing, or coding, by leveraging rhythmic work cycles.

The Pomodoro Technique

Created by Francesco Cirillo, this technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro.

How to use the Timer?

1. Add Tasks List what you want to conquer during your focus session.
2. Estimate Set your target Pomodoros (1 = 25min of pure work).
3. Start & Flow Select a task and focus intently for 25 minutes.
4. Brief Rest Take a 5-minute break when the alarm rings to recharge.
5. Iterate Repeat until the task is complete. Every 4 Pomodoros, take a longer break.
